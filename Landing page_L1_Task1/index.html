Teaching Methods

Qualified Teaching: The faculty is carefully chosen after a rigorous selection process. We aim to ensure that every faculty member has in-depth knowledge in his/her subject and teachers only in his/her discipline.
Assistance Teacher: In addition to regular classroom teachers, there are assistant faculty members who focus on providing attention to resolve doubts of students in small groups, and hand-hold them.
Attendance & Lecture timing : The Dates, Timing and duration mentioned in this prospectus are tentative. Monthly planing, Lecture duration & timing will be announce every month. Attendance is must for all lectures and tests. Daily attendance message will sent to absent students, Parents are requested to ensure their child's regularity and punctuality for the lectures and tests.
Periodical Tests and Prelims: Regular Test, based on the board examination, form a major feature of the coaching program and are scheduled right throughout the course. These periodical test will span topics, chapters and subjects. Question papers are set as per the S.S.C. Board marking scheme. Printed model answer papers, also with marking scheme, will be provided by experienced paper examiners, specialized in their fields, evaluate the test scores. Performance is monitored, both for individual-assessment and comparative rankings.



Admission Process

Admissions will be on a 'first come-first allot' basis. Student must be accompanied by their parents during the Admission process.
The following are requisites for securing admission :
1. Application form duly filled in all respects by the student and signed by the parent.
2.Two passport size colour photographs.
3.Original mark sheet of recent examination of current academic year along with a photocopy which will be retained. 4. Payment cheque(s) as required.
